Principles that guide how I design, build, and maintain software.

  • Prefer simple tools over complex frameworks
  • Command-line first — GUIs are optional
  • Understand systems before automating them
  • Make behavior explicit, not magical
  • Optimize for debuggability and longevity
  • Write software that respects constraints

I enjoy working close to the system boundary, where performance, correctness, and clarity matter more than abstraction layers.
